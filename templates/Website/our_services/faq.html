One of the key components of our company’s mission is to provide effective financial education and valuable credit learning to as many clients as possible. Ignorance or inability to understand credit essentials can hurt your financial condition in the present time as well as the future.

Get Empowered with Credit Knowledge

Repairing your credit score entails the creation of positive events and the elimination of negative entries from your credit report. Bringing positive improvements in your credit score is the fastest way of enhancing your ability to get loans, leases, credit, and mortgages. The first thing a lending financial institution or a debtor will examine is your credit history through your credit report. Below we have shortlisted some of our most frequently asked questions or client FAQs.

What is a good and a bad credit score?

As per the general principles of major Credit Bureaus, a good credit score hovers around and above 650. On the other hand, anything below 580 is considered a bad credit score.

Can we consider credit score and credit report as the same thing?

Absolutely not! A credit report is a detailed statement of your vital financial and credit information. It includes things like trade lines, account names, loan details, credit history, credit card payments, inquiries, public records, and any late or non-payment occurrences. The credit score is one of the components of this credit reports. It ranks your financial strength based on the credit events on your credit history.

What is Credit Repair?

The process in which an expert addresses the presence of questionable or negative entries on your credit report is called credit repair. If these entries are proven as unfair, inaccurate, and inauthentic, then you can get them removed as per law.

Why do I Need Credit Repair?

If you have a bad credit report and score, you are least likely to get approval for loans, mortgages, leases, or any other credit facilities. Through credit repair, you can make your credit ranking more attractive for getting all types of loans.

Is Credit Repair a legal process?

Yes, it is. It is your legal and lawful right as a citizen to get a fair chance of proving inaccuracies or wrong reporting in your credit report. You can easily challenge and offer disputes against any items you consider as negative or misquoted on your report. Your credit report should have events that are accurate, fair, and substantial.

What are the laws that govern credit repair rights?

There are two basic laws that are applicable to credit repair services and their related issues. One is called the CROA or Credit Repair Organizations Act and the second is FCRA Fair Credit Reporting Act.

In how much time can my credit be repaired or restored?

Every client has a different credit report with unique entries. Due to varying requirements, the timeframe for credit repair also varies from client to client. At Blackwater, we have a commitment to deliver significant results within 45 -60 days.

How much can my score improve with your credit repair services?

It depends on your existing credit report and score. Improvements and corrections stimulated by Blackwater may have different effects on the credit scores of different people. The key is to share your credit goals with us and we will help you accordingly.
